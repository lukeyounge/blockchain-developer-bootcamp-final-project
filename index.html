Welcome to your DIY token minting app

Quickly and easily get started with minting your own NFTs, just connect to MetaMask, select Rinkeby, then click to button to check if you are a registered minter. If not, then sign up by clicking on the 'become a minter' button.


Sign Up



You will pay a one-time fee of 0.001 eth.

Please wait a couple of minutes for your minting role to be confirmed on the blockchain. Then click on the 'Can I mint?' button above again to check if you are a minter.

Now, mint your NFT




It can take several minutes for the NFT to be confirmed on the blockchain.
